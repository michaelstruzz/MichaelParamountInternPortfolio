Introduction

Hi, my name is Michael Struzzieri. I am a former Broadcasting Streaming Specialist Intern at Paramount.

I joined Paramount in June 2025 and was there through October 2025. During that time, I had the opportunity to work closely with the company’s live-streaming and broadcast operations teams. My role involved supporting live event workflows, monitoring network feeds, and ensuring seamless broadcast delivery across multiple platforms. This experience helped me develop a strong understanding of real-time production environments, technical troubleshooting, and the evolving landscape of digital media.

I had the opportunity to connect with many amazing people who supported my growth both personally and professionally. The experience I’ve gained at Paramount was truly valuable, and I’m proud of what I’ve accomplished in my time working there.
